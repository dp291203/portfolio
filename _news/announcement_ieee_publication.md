Published and presented **"Emotion Detection in Multimodal Communication through Audio-Visual Gesture Analysis"** at **IEEE CONECCT 2024**, IISc Bangalore. The paper describes our ECAPA-TDNN audio model (97% accuracy) and LSTM gesture pipeline fused via Fuzzy Logic (87% robustness). Research conducted at Samsung R&D Institute.
